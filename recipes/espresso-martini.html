Espresso Martini

The Espresso Martini is a classic cocktail made combining Vodka, Coffee Liqueur, Sugar and Espresso.

Invented in the mid 1980s, this cocktail is said to have been created by Dick Bradsell when a famous model asked him for a drink that would "wake me up and f*** me up".

The drink is typically shaken to chill and aerate and served 'up' in a coup style glass.

Ingredients

  • 25ml Vodka
  • 25ml Kahlua
  • 10ml Sugar Syrup
  • 50ml Espresso

Steps

  1. Add the Vodka, Kahlua, Sugar Syrup and Espresso into a boston shaker.
  2. Add ice to the shaker.
  3. Shake hard for 8-10 seconds to chill and aerate the drink.
  4. Double strain the drink into a chilled coup glass.
  5. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
