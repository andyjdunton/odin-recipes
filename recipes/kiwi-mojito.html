Kiwi Mojito

A classic cuban cocktail, loved by Hemmingway. We like ours mixed up with kiwi and green tea for a refreshing twist.

The perfect mix of rum, lime, mint, kiwi and house made vanilla green tea syrup. .

The drink is typically muddled and then served long over crushed or pebble ice.

Ingredients

  • 50ml Rum
  • 25ml Lime Juice
  • 20ml Vanilla & Green Tea Syrup
  • 1/2 Kiwi Muddled
  • 8-10 Mint Leaves
  • Soda Water

Steps

  1. Add the green tea syrup, kiwi and mint leaves to a glass.
  2. Muddle to break down the kiwi and combine.
  3. Add the rum and lime juice.
  4. Half fill the glass with crushed ice and churn it up with a bar spoon.
  5. Fill up the rest of the way and then top with soda.
  6. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a slice of kiwi.
